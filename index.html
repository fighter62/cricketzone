Meet The Visionary

A Legacy of Taste
& Excellence

Bringing over a decade of international culinary experience, our founder is the visionary force behind Sangi Pizza Steak World. Renowned for mastering the art of the iconic Karachi Chaman Biryani, his journey is marked by the successful launch and management of premium hotels across Saudi Arabia and other global destinations.

His passion for exquisite flavors and unwavering commitment to luxury hospitality have driven him to establish Sangi Pizza Steak World as a premier, unparalleled dining brand. Under his leadership, every dish is a testament to global expertise meeting local tradition.
